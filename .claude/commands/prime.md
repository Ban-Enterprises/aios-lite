# Prime — Start session

> Get oriented at the start of every session.

## Instructions

Read in this order:

1. `CLAUDE.md` — workspace structure, commands
2. `.context/ROOT.md` — who is the user, priorities
3. `.context/SESSION-STATE.md` — what happened last session

## Summary

Print briefly:

1. **Who** — name, what they do
2. **Priority** — what they're working on now
3. **Last session** — what was done, what's incomplete
4. **Ready** — "I'm up to speed. What are we working on?"

Don't load extra files. Keep it short.
